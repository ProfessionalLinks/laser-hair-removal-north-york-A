How Does Laser Hair Removal Work?

Laser hair removal uses targeted light to destroy hair follicles without damaging the surrounding skin. The treatment focuses on the melanin in the hair, making it an ideal option for individuals with darker hair and lighter skin tones. However, advancements in technology have made it suitable for a wider range of hair and skin types.

Advantages of Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal offers numerous benefits, making it a superior choice over traditional methods:

  • * Effective for both small and large treatment areas


  • * Minimal recovery time and side effects


  • * Reduces the risk of ingrown hairs, common with shaving and waxing


  • * Smoother skin with less irritation over time

During the treatment, the laser technician will use a handheld device to target the hair follicles in the designated area. The sensation is often described as a mild stinging or snapping. Post-treatment care is crucial to achieving the best results. You may experience slight redness or swelling, but this typically subsides within a few hours.
